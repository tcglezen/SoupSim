Final Project: SoupSim

Thomas Glezen, Amy Liu, Joshua Yang, Katelyn Biesiadecki



Summary

Pretty Soup simulates the physical properties of soup, including viscosity, textures, and clumps of various ingredients within the soup. With Pretty Soup, users can tinker with various soupie parameters to construct their ideal soup and interact with it however they like within a virtual environment. Pretty Soup represents a small but important step toward the metaverse that we are building as virtual dreamers of the future.

Problem Description

In many video games and animations, soup is generally just a colored liquid. Oftentimes, gamers and audiences may find this unappetizing and a sneaky but potent reminder that virtual worlds are indeed not real. Even when soup is carefully crafted by animators, they have to painstakingly animate individual clumps in the soup and keyframe various aspects of the soup that could make the process very tedious. Thus, with SoupSim, we hope to build a fluid simulator with customizable viscosity and the option of extra components within the fluids to amplify the quality, realism, and accessibility of top quality soup in virtual worlds within video games, animated films, commercials, and more.
